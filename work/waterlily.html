Project Overview
Waterily is a modular typeface created from a grid system that is made of different sizes of circles and semi-circles. The Typeface aims to imitate the curvilinear visual quality of petals and leaves.
Leaves and Petals
The graphics shown were the result of experimenting with the rounded corner tool in Adobe Illustrator, which served as the initial inspiration for the typeface.
The Grid
The grid system consists of overlapped circles of two different sizes and a standard square grid.
