2022
Credits
Luckin Coffee digital marketing team
My Role
Throughout my internship, I designed numerous collections of posters and illustrations that were customized for social media use and were strategically created to align with specific dates, events, and platforms.
Project Overview
Luckin Coffee is a Chinese coffeehouse chain that aims to provide accessible, affordable, and diverse coffee products for its customers.
‍
As a marketing intern at Luckin Coffee, I worked closely with the digital marketing team to generate artistic materials that would be used in the company's various marketing campaigns.
Fortune of the Month
Fortune of the Month is a collection of illustrations that I created for the Weibo platform. Each post includes a one-time GIF that, when clicked on, displays a line predicting the user's fortune for the upcoming month.

(Fortune of January: grow thicker hair)
Holiday Illustration
I created various sets of wallpapers and avatar photos for customers to use on their cell phones on Luckin Coffee's social media platforms. These images were often themed around a specific holiday or festival and were incorporated into marketing campaigns.
Membership Card
The task of the project was to design membership cards that target different usage scenarios for different customer segments, including office workers before and during work, students in between classes, and large group purchases. The membership cards are called by a joint name Luckin Free Card, meaning the member has the freedom to purchase coffee at anywhere anytime. In the designs, different characters represent different customer segments, while the variations of the background colors in blue illustrate different usage scenarios, including daytime and night.
New Year Calendar
The New Year Calendar series was a collection of calendar-based posters that linked various drinks to the first seven days of the Chinese New Year. The posters also featured predictions for New Year fortunes.
