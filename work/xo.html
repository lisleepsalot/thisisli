Project Overview
"XO aims to serve as a platform for the examination and exploration of modern romantic and sexual landscapes through creative outlets of expression. We focus on highlighting marginalized voices, identities, and experiences as we work to engage the larger Brown, RISD, and Providence communities in discussions about love, sex, and sexuality."

The Logo
The XO logo and visuals are inspired by the shape of puzzle pieces that can be assembled organically and fit tightly together, which is meant to represent the organization's mission of highlighting marginalized voices, identities, and experiences.
The logo was designed with a curvilinear outline to avoid the use of sharp corners in a 3x3 grid.
The Puzzle Visuals
The puzzle visuals adopted the same principle of avoiding corners.
The puzzle pieces were created using a uniform visual system in which the outline of the pieces can be freely disassembled and assembled together.
The Puzzles' Outline
The puzzle pieces were designed to eliminate straight lines while maintaining the recognizable puzzle shape. This lack of straight lines allows the pieces to fit together in a more organic and curvilinear way.
The three main variations of the design include the logo alone, the logo with the puzzle pieces, and the puzzle pieces alone.
The first issue of Spring 2023 featuring the new visual system is slated for release in late March 2023. Stay tuned for updates!
