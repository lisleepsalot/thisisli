Atlas Swings

Game Design
2023
Credits
Atlas Swings team 2023
My Role
As the artist and graphic designer of the team, I collaborated with my team to research and explore art styles and game flow. I devised and refined UI elements, produced particle effects, and created 3D assets. Additionally, I worked closely with the programmers to import assets into the game.
Project Overview
"CLANGGG!!!
A thunderous thud rings through Rockefeller Square and New York City. Atlas, the statue who holds it all together, has lost the globe again. With a lamp post by his side, time for sad-dad-statue's favorite Sunday morning activity: golfing the globe back to its place.."

As an entry in the 2023 Geopipe NYC game jam, Atlas Swings is a golf game built using the Geopipe-provided model of New York City. The game won first place in the game jam.

Adam Ying's opening scene (slide 2) won the award for the 2D animation category at the JoyArt Boston 2023 Game Art competition.

The game is availavle here.
3D Models
In addition to fixing and retexturing the provided models, my main duty for 3D models was to create vehicle models to be used as obstacles in the game. (The first sedan model was created by Beatty Fulmina as a reference.)
Particle Effect
Using the cover art style and color palette created by Nicole Zhu as a reference, I created two particle effects for the game in Unity.
UI Design
As the game's protagonist is the Greek god Atlas (in front of Rockefeller Square), the UI design drew inspiration from Greek pottery, featuring an orange and dark green silhouette style.
The Game
