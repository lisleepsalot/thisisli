Project Overview
This project is the final assignment for RISD Design Studio 1 in graphic design, taught by Nancy Skolos.
The Process
In this assignment, we started by playing “PingPong” with a partner, which involved working on a single composition back and forth to create dynamic, experimental visual elements. Then, individually, we retrieved a visual inventory of colors, forms, and compositions from the process and expanded upon it as a visual system. The Donuts on Sticks element came from composition step No. 43 of PingPong between me and Brian Yoo. I adopted the combination of circles and diagonal lines in the image and refined the colors into CMYK colors, creating a prototype of the basic Donuts on Sticks visual element. Using this basic building block, I have created a series of posters and flyers for several imaginary music festivals.
