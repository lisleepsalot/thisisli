Credits
BRGD Meowfia team
My Role
I worked with other level designers and artists to create background art and character designs that adhere to the game vibe.
Project Overview
Meowfia is a 2D pixel art game built with unity. “In a two-player game of cat and house, one attempts to escape the devious traps of the humans, the other STOPS THEM.” The game adopted a flat, cartoonish aesthetic.
The game is available here.
Character Design
The character setting of the cat was a mafia boss with a scar on his face, and I had to create the character in a 12 x 16 pixels frame.
The Characters Finalist
The team has chosen a light color palette that contrasts well on a dark background for the characters. Among the characters I created, the team decided on a draft that maximizes head size for facial expressions and simplifies limbs for animations.
The Background
The levels in Meofia were assigned different themes. I was responsible for the background art of dark academia and the 1970s theme. The background art is composed of a base background layer and seperate interactive items.
