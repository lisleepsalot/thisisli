My Role
Despite receiving input from other designers and support from the client manager in communicating with the client, I was the only designer directly involved in this project.
Project Overview
KiraKira is a car detailing studio that aims to provide vibrant, personalized, and professional car-care services for the urban youth in Shanghai. The name “KiraKira” comes from the Japanese word キラキラ, which means glittery.

The objective of the project was to update the logo design and accompanying visuals and accessories, ensuring they align with the studio's identity.
The Color Palette
The updated logo (right) features a lighter and less contrasting version of the neon color palette used in the previous logo design (left). The new logo also utilizes a stroke-heavy art style that alludes to a graffiti texture, evoking a sense of urban energy and vitality.

The KIRA2 Graphics
The Kira²  visual is a simpler representation of KiraKira with geometric compositions and a limited color palette. This visual is intended to be printed on surfaces where complex color compositions are difficult to process like canvas and metal.
